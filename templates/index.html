Project Scope

Many music streaming platforms leverage genre as a valuable datapoint. Given the volume of content available, genre is a good criteria to expose subscribers to:

  • related artists or tracks
  • previously unexplored, existing content
  • newly released content

Classifying songs by genre is a challenging exercise. Over time, as style and culture change, the list of genres and subgenre expand. Often, an individual song matches different genres.

This project aims to:

Data Exploration

Analyze a Kaggle dataset containing 160,000+ Spotify songs

View details »

Modeling

Explore various Machine Learning models and create a model to predict the genre of a song based on its audio features

View details »

Predict

Evaluate the model against recently released music and assess its accuracy.
