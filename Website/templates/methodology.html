Methodology

Data Gathering

Kaggle hosts a dataset of 160,000+ songs obtained from the Spotify API spanning 1921 to 2020. This dataset contains various metadata including:

  • artist
  • release date
  • track title
  • Spotify track ID
  • various audio features

Spotify API associates the genre field with the artist. As such, an artist can have content matching multiple genres (e.g. dance pop, latin, miami hip hop, pop, pop rap). The tracks from this artists are then inherit the associated genres.

Spotify Audio Features

Here is a description of the various audio features captured for each track:

acousticness danceability duration_ms energy instrumentalness key liveness loudness mode speechicness tempo time_signature valence
acousticness

A confidence measure from 0.0 to 1.0 of whether the track is acoustic. 1.0 represents high confidence the track is acoustic.

danceability

how suitable a track is for dancing based on a combination of musical elements including tempo, rhythm stability, beat strength, and overall regularity. A value of 0.0 is least danceable and 1.0 is most danceable.

duration

Duration of the track in milliseconds

energy

Energy is a measure from 0.0 to 1.0 and represents a perceptual measure of intensity and activity. Typically, energetic tracks feel fast, loud, and noisy.

instrumentalness

Predicts whether a track contains no vocals. Rap or spoken word tracks are clearly “vocal”. The closer the instrumentalness value is to 1.0, the greater likelihood the track contains no vocal content.

key

Estimated overall key of the track (e.g 0 = C, 1 = C♯/D♭, 2 = D)

liveness

Detects the presence of an audience in the recording. A value above 0.8 provides strong likelihood that the track is live.

loudness

The overall loudness of a track in decibels (dB).

mode

The type of scale from which its melodic content is derived. Major is represented by 1 and minor is 0.

speechicness

Speechiness detects the presence of spoken words in a track. The more exclusively speech-like the recording (e.g. talk show, audio book, poetry), the closer to 1.0 the attribute value.

tempo

The overall estimated tempo of a track in beats per minute (BPM).

time_signature

Number of beats in a bar.

valence

A measure from 0.0 to 1.0 describing the musical positiveness conveyed by a track. Tracks with high valence sound more positive (e.g. happy, cheerful, euphoric), while tracks with low valence sound more negative (e.g. sad, depressed, angry).
